What we Will do In
Upcoming Future

Living Robots

Researchers working in Top Universities have already developed the first example of living robots the world has seen. The team adapted stem cells from frogs to create tiny "xenobots" just a single millimeter in size. So far, they have programmed them to swim and pick up items, which points to varius powerful applications in future robotic technology. It helps to remove the plastic particles from the ocean.

Flying Cars

Cars that can fly are set to be accessible to the public as soon as 2024. Uber is planning to tria a vertical take-off and landing vehicle developed by Hyundai in Los Angeles and Melbourne. It can travel in 60 miles from a single charge and fully recharge its batteries in just 7 minutes. Other firms like Toyota and Google share similar future Technnology predictions and are developing their own flying cars.

Data Driven Economy

The data-driven economy and insights-driven organizations signal a transformative shift, placing data at the heart of business startegies and society. Enhanced technologies like IoT systems, extensive ERP's large tracking systems and AI amplify the value and accessibility of data. These tools facilitate real time data collection and empowering business to tailor their products while improving customer experiences.

Digital Trust

To create a safer space for digital users, cybersecurity and ethical hacking are the major specializations you can check out. In these two, there is an array of jobs you can discover from junior to senior levels. For ethical hacking, you might have to take up professional certifications, while for cybersecurity, a diploma or even a master’s qualification is sufficient to aim for a high-salary.

3D Printing

A key trend in innovation and technology is 3D printing which is used to formulate prototypes. This technology has been impactful in the biomedical and industrial sectors. None of us thought of printing a real object from a printer, while right now, it’s a reality. So, 3D printing is another innovation that’s here to stay. For companies in the data and healthcare sector that require a lot of 3D printing for their products, various jobs pay well and are international.

Genomics

Imagine a technology that can study your DNA and use it to improve your health, helping you fight diseases and whatnot! Genomics is precisely that technology that peruses upon the make-up of genes, DNAs, their mapping, structure, etc. Further, this can help quantify your genes and result in finding diseases or any possible problems that can later be a health issue. When it comes to a specialization like Genomics, one can find a variety of technical as well as non-technical roles.
